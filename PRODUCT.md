# Product

## Register

product

## Users

NEU Study Admin is used by teaching staff, administrators, and authorized users who manage study operations: courses, categories, knowledge points, questions, papers, practice sessions, grading, users, roles, permissions, and activity records. They are usually in a task-focused admin workflow and need the interface to feel responsive, predictable, and easy to scan.

## Product Purpose

The product centralizes teaching administration for NEU study workflows. Success means users can find the right module quickly, understand system state at a glance, and complete management, review, and grading tasks without friction.

## Brand Personality

Calm, efficient, trustworthy. Motion should be visible and satisfying, but tied to state changes, navigation, feedback, loading, and hierarchy.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page decoration, page-load choreography that delays work, ornamental motion, heavy glass effects, oversized hero treatment, and inconsistent component behavior across screens.

## Design Principles

State should be felt immediately.

Navigation should keep users oriented.

Dense information should remain calm and scannable.

Interactive controls should acknowledge input with clear feedback.

Motion should make the product feel alive without slowing the task.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard focus quality. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` with simplified transitions while keeping the standard experience animated for users who want motion.
